About Karnataka

Karnataka is a state in southern India known for its diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant cities. Whether you're exploring the bustling streets of Bangalore, the serene backwaters of Coorg, or the ancient ruins of Hampi, Karnataka offers a unique and unforgettable experience.

Top Attractions

Mysore Palace

Explore the grandeur of Mysore Palace, a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Hampi

Visit the ancient city of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ruins and temples.

Coorg

Experience the serene beauty of Coorg, famous for its coffee plantations and scenic landscapes.

Jog Falls

Witness the breathtaking beauty of Jog Falls, the second highest plunge waterfall in India.

Chikmagalur

Enjoy the coffee plantations and scenic views in the hill station of Chikmagalur.

Gokarna

Relax on the pristine beaches of Gokarna, a popular pilgrimage destination.
